04 Research Themes
  • T.01 Human Agency in AI Designing AI systems that augment rather than replace human decision-making. Studying how interface design shapes perceived autonomy and control in collaborative workflows.
  • T.02 AI in Education How LLM-based tools reshape learning, critical thinking, and student autonomy in real-world K-12 and university contexts. Field studies, not lab studies.
  • T.03 Digital Resilience Building literacy and protective capabilities for vulnerable populations — older adults, young learners — navigating AI-generated misinformation and deepfakes.
